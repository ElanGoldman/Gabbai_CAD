# Replacement parts for our megilah cases

The OCP has several מגילות that were donated over the years. While the scrolls are all כושר, two of them had damaged cases.

One was a nice woden case where the bottom was cracked and a piece was missing. I 3d printed a replacement bottom to fit it. This could probably be extended to a full case if anyone is interested in a 3d printed מגילה case.

The second was a 3 inch diameter acrylic tube without a top or bottom. I 3d printed stoppers for the top and bottom of the tube.
