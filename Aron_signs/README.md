# Signs for opening and closing the aron with strings

Our פרוכת opened with strings. Pull one string to open and the other to close. These stings looked identical and people would ofter pull the wrong one leading to delays in פתיחה and embarrassment. I made these signs to easily distinguish the two strings. They also serve as handles to pull the strings.

I recomend printing them in defferent colors (I did green for open, red for close) to help distinguish them.
